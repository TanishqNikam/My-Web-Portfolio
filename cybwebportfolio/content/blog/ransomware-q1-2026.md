## Overview
Recent trends suggest ransomware operators are rapidly adapting to standard detection mechanisms. 

### Key Findings
- **Phishing**: Still the number one initial access vector, though significantly more targeted (Spear-phishing).
- **Vulnerability Exploitation**: Unpatched perimeter devices (VPNs, firewalls) constitute the second most common entry point.

### Recommendations
1. Patch external infrastructure within 48 hours of critical CVE releases.
2. Ensure offline, immutable backups are maintained.
3. Run regular simulated phishing campaigns for all company employees.
